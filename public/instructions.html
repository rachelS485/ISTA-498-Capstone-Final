This tool will help you plan out your four year academic career at the University of Arizona


After logging in, you are brought to a screen where you can choose to explore 4 different options. The four are Create a 4-year plan, Interest Survey, Course Reccommendation, and Resource Links.





First, you should click on Interest survey because it is a REQUIREMENT that you fill that out to get course reccommendations in the reccommendations tab.



In the interest survey, you must first select your current major empahsis. Once done, you'll be able to give a repsonse to the rest of the questions.





After you complete the survey, the reccommendations tab will fill up. You can take a look at that to get and idea of what classes you want to put in your 4 year planner.


Once you've chosen what classes you want to take, it's time to put them in your planner. After you've made your way to the course planner tab, you can choose to add any class to one of the semesters.





To add the class, type the name of the course where it says "Type here.." and then click "Add" to add it.





Additionally, you can click on Resource Links for helpful references as well as instructions on how to use D2L and UAccess
